Platform Features

Everything a student pilot needs to study with structure.

PreFlight brings your aviation learning into one focused space: book-led lessons, guided modules, progress tracking, and social motivation that keeps study moving without feeling scattered.

Book-led lessons Study from organized aviation references with clear module paths.
Progress tracking See completed lessons, accuracy, streaks, and weak areas.
Quiz practice Review topics through focused checks before moving ahead.
Social motivation Learn with friends, compare progress, and stay consistent.
Student Dashboard Know exactly what to study next.

The dashboard keeps lessons, quiz status, streaks, and review tasks in one clear cockpit view.

  • Continue the current PPL lesson
  • Track module progress and weak areas
  • Keep daily study streaks visible
Quiz Practice Check understanding while lessons are still fresh.

Quizzes sit beside the lesson flow, so student pilots can answer, review explanations, and return to weak topics without losing their place.

  • Answer focused module checks
  • Review correct and missed choices
  • Move between lessons and quizzes smoothly
